About

I'm Isaac Shivvers, a graduate student in astrophysics, and this website shows time-lapse videos taken by the webcams at Lick Observatory during the course of my Ph.D studies in the astronomy department at UC Berkeley.

There is one video per day, organized by month (a total of {{n}} months), excepting the rare days when videos were not saved for technical issues. You may notice that some videos show static or other problems. These camera feeds are not the scientific observations from the telescope - instead they are used by the scientists at Lick Observatory to monitor the weather (many scientists operate the telescopes remotely) and to archive a record of the cloud cover during the night's scientific observations.

I built this page as an homage to Lick and to the Shane telescope - the source of most of the data I worked with as a Ph.D student and a remarkable scientific tool that I am grateful to have used on so many sleepless nights.

-Isaac Shivvers
